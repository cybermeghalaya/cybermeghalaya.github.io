About

Cyber Wing is an unit of Meghalaya Police dedicated to cyber crime and cyber security.

Functions of Cyber Wing

  • To investigate cyber-crime cases of general importance.
  • To function as Computer Emergency Response Team for Meghalaya Police.
  • To develop, maintain, and secure digital infrastructure of Meghalaya Police.
  • To impart training in cyber-crime investigation to police personnel and generate awareness among citizens.
  • To assist all units in the prevention and investigation of cyber-crime.
  • To coordinate with Indian Cyber Crime Coordination Centre (I4C), cyber-crime nodal of other states, nodal heads of intermediaries, and other such agencies.
  • To acquire cyber forensic capability and conduct forensic examination of digital evidence.
  • To undertake research and development in the field of cyber-crime investigation and digital forensics.
  • To implement all schemes in the domain of cyber-crime investigation and prevention.
  • Any other function as assigned by the Director General of Police (DGP).
