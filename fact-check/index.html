Fact check

Published fact checks Verified hotels in Meghalaya

The Cyber Wing has a fact checking unit which conducts fact checks of certain types of suspicious information which includes:

  1. Rumours on social media.
  2. Suspicious communications from law enforcement agencies intending to commit fraud.
  3. Impersonation of Government functionaries
  4. Malicoius mobile applications and websites
  5. Ponzi investment schemes on cyber space

You may request fact checking by Cyber Wing, Meghalaya Police before conducting transactions with unknown persons.

For making request, email us at ccw-meg@gov.in with subject as Request for fact checking.
