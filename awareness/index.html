Awareness

In today’s interconnected world, the internet has become an essential part of our daily lives, revolutionizing the way we communicate, work, and conduct business. While this digital transformation brings numerous benefits, it also opens the door to new threats—cyber crime being one of the most pervasive. Cyber crime encompasses a wide range of illegal activities carried out using technology, from identity theft and financial fraud to malicious attacks on critical infrastructure.
